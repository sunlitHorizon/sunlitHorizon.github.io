HOW TO SPEAK COTTONESE

--official!--


the basics

Cottonese is just english, but every word is reversed, but not the order.

Example:

Olleh drlow! √

Drlow olleh! X


Notice that √ means correct, and X means incorrect.


Now, what about a URL? An acronym? A name?

The answer is simple. Don't reverse them.


Examples:

Eht URL ot siht etisbew si sunlitHorizon.github.io dna ti sgnoleb ot sunlitHorizøn. √

Eht LRU ot siht etisbew si oi.buhtig.noziroHtilnus dna ti sgnolrb ot nøziroHtilnus. X



And what about slang?

Reverse it. Why, if it's acronyms? Because it's basically used like words.


Punctuation?

Keep it in the original order.


Examples:

T'nod nur rm -rf /* --no-preserve-root ! √

Tno'd nur rm -rf /* --no-preserve-root ! X
